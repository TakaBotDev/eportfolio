Engineering course

Scientific or engineering-related school projects completed so far.

2018 - Apprenti-chercheur

I had the opportunity to join a research team at the Jacques Monod Institute, working on a project that analyzed fly behavior before and after mating. Through this experience, I discovered what a typical day in a laboratory is like and learned that research is, above all, a matter of patience and communication. Together with another student researcher, I presented our work at a scientific conference — a truly rewarding experience that helped me gain confidence, as I used to be quite shy.

2024 - TIPE

I aimed to model the renal system to highlight the effects of doping on its functioning, as part of my TIPE project on Games and Sports. I tested a simplified model simulating the functioning of the renal system and analyzed the results using my knowledge of fluid mechanics and my research on renal physiology.

2025 - TIPE

I worked on the design of a self-powered seismograph as part of my TIPE project on Transition, Transformation, Conversion. My goal was to use a piezoelectric material as an accelerometer to detect vibrations. Since a piezoelectric material can generate electrical impulses when subjected to mechanical stress, it was possible to design a self-powered accelerometer — and therefore, a self-powered seismograph.
